Which grass should I plant?

Answer a few questions about your site and how you'll use the lawn. You'll get a Virginia-adapted grass recommendation, what to look for on the seed bag at the store, and how much to buy — all from VCE turfgrass guidance.

Built from VCE sources: SPES-748 "Selecting Turfgrass for Virginia," the Extension Master Gardener Handbook chapter 11, and the lawn maintenance calendars (430-520/522/523). Variety details: VCE SPES-617 (updated yearly). No brands endorsed — your seed bag's label is always the authority.
